# Product Context

## Why This Product Exists
Little Stars website serves as the digital presence for a Bakirkoy-based kindergarten. It aims to attract parents, provide information about programs, and enable online registration.

## Problems It Solves
- Parents need to find reliable childcare information online
- School needs an attractive web presence to compete
- Streamlines the registration process

## User Experience Goals
- Feel safe, joyful, and child-friendly
- Easy navigation for busy parents
- Clear program information by age group
- Simple registration and tour booking process
- Trust signals: MEB approval, ISO certification, testimonials
